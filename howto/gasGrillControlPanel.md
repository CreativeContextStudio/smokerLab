# Weber Spirit II – Control Panel Reference

Compact reference for zone patterns, burner positions, and safety on the E-210 and E-310.

***

## Spirit II anatomy

| Component | Location | Function |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Burner knobs** | Front panel (2 on E-210, 3 on E-310) | Left/center/right. Each controls one burner lane. |
| **Igniter button** | Front panel | Matchless ignition; clicks spark to first burner turned to HIGH. |
| **Flavorizer bars** | Above burners, below grate | Angled stainless bars; vaporize drippings for flavor, reduce flare-ups. |
| **Cast iron grates** | Cooking surface | Porcelain-enameled, high thermal mass, retain heat for good searing. |
| **Warming rack** | Above main grate | ~105 sq in; good for buns, resting meat, indirect warming. |
| **Lid thermometer** | Lid center | Reads air temp at grate height. Useful for overall temperature; not a grate-surface probe. |
| **Grease management** | Below burners | Funnel system drains to catch pan. Keep the catch pan clean. |

***

## Zone patterns — E-310 (3 burners: left / center / right)

| Pattern | Left | Center | Right | Lid Therm | Use For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Full HIGH** | HIGH | HIGH | HIGH | 550°F+ | Burgers, shrimp, searing, preheat |
| **Two-zone sear** | HIGH | OFF | OFF | 450–500°F | Reverse sear, pork chops, chicken thighs |
| **Two-zone buffer** | HIGH | LOW | OFF | 400–450°F | Sausage, hoagies, kabobs with cooling zone |
| **Full MEDIUM** | MED | MED | MED | 375–425°F | Veggie kebabs, corn, flatbread, fruit |
| **Indirect roast** | LOW-MED | OFF | LOW-MED | 325–375°F | Spatchcock chicken, whole chicken quarters |
| **Max sear strip** | HIGH | HIGH | LOW | 500–550°F | Steak crust, fish steaks, lamb chops |

***

## Zone patterns — E-210 (2 burners: left / right)

| Pattern | Left | Right | Lid Therm | Use For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Full HIGH** | HIGH | HIGH | 550°F+ | Burgers, seafood, preheat, searing |
| **Two-zone** | HIGH | OFF | 400–500°F | Steaks (reverse sear), pork chops, chicken |
| **Full MEDIUM** | MED | MED | 375–425°F | Kebabs, veggies, corn, bread |
| **Indirect** | LOW-MED | OFF | 300–350°F | Roasting, gentle finish |

***

## Burner knob positions and heat output

| Setting | Approximate temperature directly over burner |
| --- | --- |
| HIGH | 550–650°F |
| MEDIUM-HIGH | 450–550°F |
| MEDIUM | 375–425°F |
| LOW | 300–350°F |
| OFF | Ambient (indirect zone) |

The lid thermometer reads 50–75°F below grate surface temperature at HIGH. Use an instant-read thermometer for all meat doneness decisions.

***

## Preheat checklist

- [ ] Lid open before lighting
- [ ] All burners HIGH after first confirms lit
- [ ] Lid closed for 10–15 minutes
- [ ] Lid thermometer 500°F+
- [ ] Brush grates (after preheat, not before)
- [ ] Dial burners to cooking pattern
- [ ] Oil the food, not the grates

***

## Safety quick-reference

| Action | Rule |
| --- | --- |
| **Before lighting** | Open lid. All knobs OFF. Open tank valve slowly. |
| **Leak check** | Soapy water on hose + connections. Bubbles = leak, stop and fix. |
| **Ignition failure** | Wait 1 minute, try again. Use match through side hole if needed. |
| **After every cook** | Close tank valve. Let grill cool. Cover when cool. |
| **Gas smell** | Turn off all burners. Close tank valve. Move away. Do not light. |
| **Flare-up** | Keep lid open. Move food to indirect zone. Lower or kill burner. Never use water. |

***

## Common temperature setups

**Smash burgers (450–500°F):**
All burners HIGH → preheat 10–15 min → cook lid open

**Reverse-sear ribeye (indirect 350°F → sear 500°F+):**
E-310: right OFF (indirect) → left HIGH (sear). Move steak from right to left for finish.
E-210: right OFF (indirect) → left HIGH (sear). Same move.

**Chicken thighs (350–375°F):**
E-310: left MEDIUM-HIGH, right LOW. Start skin-down on left, finish on right.
E-210: left MEDIUM-HIGH, right LOW. Same.

**Spatchcock chicken quarters (375°F indirect):**
E-310: left LOW-MEDIUM, center OFF, right OFF. All chicken on right/center. Crisp on left at HIGH.
E-210: left LOW-MEDIUM, right OFF. Chicken on right. Crisp on left at HIGH.
