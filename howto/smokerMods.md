# CC1830S Smoker Mods & Setup

Do this once and you're set.

---

## Step 1: Seal doors and seams

1. Clean surfaces (degrease, dry).
2. Apply the **gasket tape** to:
   - The lip of the main cook chamber door.
   - The firebox door lip. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z3Mnl-HHrgs)
3. Apply **RTV silicone** along:
   - The seam where the firebox bolts to the cook chamber.
   - Any other visible gaps around that joint. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z3Mnl-HHrgs)
4. Let cure per instructions before firing up. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z3Mnl-HHrgs)

## Baffle & tuning plate layout

Think of the cook chamber as a sideways rectangle:

- **Left side:** Firebox connection.
- **Right side:** Chimney/exhaust stack.

Heat and smoke enter from the firebox, travel under a baffle/plates, rise and move across the grate, then exit the stack.

### DIY baffle / tuning plate layout

Inside the main chamber, under the cooking grates:

1. **Immediately next to the firebox opening (left side):**
   - Place a **flat steel plate or heavy pizza pan** just above the opening, extending 8"–12" into the chamber. [ddrbbqsupply](https://ddrbbqsupply.com/collections/tuning-plates-for-offset-smokers-heat-control-even-cooking)
   - It should partially block the direct blast of heat so it goes under and along the bottom instead of straight onto the grate.

2. **Across the rest of the chamber bottom (toward the stack):**
   - Lay 2–3 pieces of flat steel or similar plates side‑by‑side under the grate, leaving small adjustable gaps between them. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8otOcXiyB8U)
   - Start with the plates closer together near the firebox side and slightly more spaced toward the stack side.

### Layout diagram

```
[FIREBOX] | [BAFFLE PLATE covering entry] | [TUNING PLATE 1][gap][TUNING PLATE 2][gap][TUNING PLATE 3] | [STACK]
```

This makes the firebox side less nuclear and brings the stack side up so temps are closer across the grate. [ddrbbqsupply](https://ddrbbqsupply.com/collections/tuning-plates-for-offset-smokers-heat-control-even-cooking)
