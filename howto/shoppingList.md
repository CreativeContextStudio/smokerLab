# Smoker Shopping List

Budget-friendly upgrades for the **Royal Gourmet CC1830S offset smoker**. You don't need these exact brands, but this style/spec is what you want. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z3Mnl-HHrgs)

---

## 1. Door gasket (for lid + firebox)

- **Type:** High‑temp smoker gasket tape, fiberglass, self‑adhesive, rated to at least ~800–1000°F. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0oSNDGDh3go)
- **Spec:**
  - Width: about 1/2"–3/4"
  - Thickness: about 1/8"
  - Length: 15–30 ft roll (enough for cook chamber door and firebox door)
- **Use:** Stick around the main chamber door and firebox door to stop leaks and improve temp control. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z3Mnl-HHrgs)

Search phrase: **"LavaLock high temp BBQ gasket 1/2 inch x 1/8 inch"**. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0oSNDGDh3go)

---

## 2. High‑temp RTV sealant (for seams)

- **Type:** Food‑safe, high‑temperature RTV silicone, rated to around 600°F or higher. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z3Mnl-HHrgs)
- **Use on the CC1830S:**
  - Seams where the firebox meets the cook chamber.
  - Any bolt holes or obvious gaps that leak smoke (but not the inside of the stack). [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xQ2I_jjAMA8)

Search phrase: **"red high temp RTV silicone gasket maker 650°F"**. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0oSNDGDh3go)

---

## 3. Charcoal basket

- **Type:** Rectangular or square **charcoal basket** made from expanded metal, sized to fit in a small offset firebox. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xQ2I_jjAMA8)
- **Target dimensions:** Roughly 10"–12" long, 8"–10" wide, 4"–6" tall so it sits comfortably in the CC1830S firebox with air around it. [lowes](https://www.lowes.com/pd/Royal-Gourmet-26-77-in-W-Black-Barrel-Charcoal-Grill/5014685623)
- **Features:**
  - Elevated bottom (or legs) to let ash fall through.
  - Sturdy expanded metal sides so charcoal stays bunched together. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xQ2I_jjAMA8)

Search phrase: **"charcoal basket for small offset smoker expanded metal"**. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xQ2I_jjAMA8)

If you can't find a perfect fit, you can also use a **small charcoal "Vortex" basket** or have expanded metal bent into a simple rectangle.

---

## 4. Digital thermometer (grate + meat)

- **Type:** Dual‑probe (or 4‑probe) digital BBQ thermometer with cables rated for smoker temps. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xQ2I_jjAMA8)
- **Features to look for:**
  - At least one **grate probe** (attach with a small clip or through a potato/onion) and one **meat probe**.
  - Cables rated to ~700°F.
  - Basic alarm and continuous readout. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xQ2I_jjAMA8)

Search phrase: **"dual probe BBQ thermometer smoker 700F probe"**. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xQ2I_jjAMA8)

---

## 5. Optional but recommended

- **Chimney starter:** Standard metal chimney to light charcoal without lighter fluid. Search: **"charcoal chimney starter large"**.
- **Disposable aluminum pans:** 9"×13" pans for water/drip under meat and for catching firebox ash.
- **Lump charcoal + small splits or chunks:**
  - Lump or briquettes as your base fuel.
  - Wood chunks or small splits of hickory, oak, or pecan for smoke.
