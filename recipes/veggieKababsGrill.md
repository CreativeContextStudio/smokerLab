**Step‑by‑step guide** for **veggie kebabs**, tuned for **zucchini, peppers, onions, mushrooms** over **direct medium heat**. Total time: **10–15 minutes active grilling** after preheat, charred edges, tender insides.

***

## 1. Prep the veggie kebabs (15–30 minutes before grilling)

- **Choose the veggies:**  
  - 2 medium **zucchini** (1/2" thick rounds).  
  - 2–3 **bell peppers** (1" chunks).  
  - 1 large **red onion** (1" wedges).  
  - 8–12 **cremini or button mushrooms** (whole or halved).  
- **Seasoning:**  
  - Olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, dried oregano or Italian herbs.  

**Do’s**  
- Cut veggies into **1–1.5" pieces** (uniform for even cooking).  
- Toss in bowl: 3 tbsp oil + 1 tsp salt + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp oregano.  
- Thread onto **metal skewers** (2–3 types of veg per skewer, not overcrowded).  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t use super‑soft veggies (cherry tomatoes); they fall off.  
- Don’t oil too heavily; drips cause flare‑ups.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Direct **medium (375–425°F)**.
- **Fire setup:**
  - **Charcoal pan in mid position.**
  - **Full bed** of charcoal across the grate (1–1.5 chimney loads, even layer). [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Mostly open** for steady airflow. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 10–15 minutes** with lid closed until lid thermometer reads ~400°F.
- Brush grates clean while hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t preheat too long; veggies cook fast.
- Don’t use two‑zone; even medium heat chars all sides.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Direct **medium (375–425°F)**.
- **Weber Spirit II setup:**
  - **E‑310:** All three burners MEDIUM‑HIGH.
  - **E‑210:** Both burners MEDIUM‑HIGH.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners MEDIUM‑HIGH, lid closed **10–15 min** until ~400°F.
- **Oil the skewers**, not the grates.

**Do’s**
- Drippings from oiled veggies hit Flavorizer bars and vaporize, adding flavor similar to charcoal.
- Use a grill basket as an alternative to skewers for small veggie pieces.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t use HIGH; scorches mushrooms and onions before peppers soften.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the kebabs on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Direct over coals** across the hot grate.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - Lay skewers flat, **not overlapping**.  

**Do’s**  
- Grill **3–4 minutes per side** (2–3 rotations total).  
- Turn with **long tongs**; look for char marks and softened texture.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overcrowd; steaming kills char.  
- Don’t flip too often; let marks develop.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (375–425°F direct)

**Goal:** Charred edges, tender but crisp veggies. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Phase | Time | Visual cue |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Side 1** | 3–4 min | Char marks, peppers softening. |
| **Side 2** | 3–4 min | Onions caramelizing, zucchini tender. |
| **Side 3 (optional rotate)** | 2–3 min | Even char all around. |

**Do’s**  
- Check after first flip; pull when charred and tender.  
- No thermometer needed; fork‑tender is cue.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t let onions burn black; pull early if needed.  
- Don’t sauce on grill; burns fast.  

***

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest off heat **1–2 minutes** (minimal needed).  
- Serve with **chimichurri, ranch, or your smoked salsa** + lemon wedges.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t over‑rest; veggies cool fast.  
- Don’t cover tightly; keeps char crisp.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for veggie kebabs on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **full medium grate** and **lid thermometer** to confirm ~400°F.  
- Uniform 1–1.5" pieces, oil lightly.  
- Grill **lid open** for char control.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip preheat or dry veggies; steaming kills char.  
- Don’t overcrowd skewers; needs space.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **4–6 kebabs:** Shave 1–2 min.  
- **12+ kebabs:** Cook in 2 batches.  

Charred, smoky, veggie perfection.
