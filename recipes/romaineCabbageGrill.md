**Step‑by‑step guide** for **grilled romaine or cabbage wedges**, tuned for **2 heads romaine or 1 medium cabbage** over **direct high heat**. Total time: **4–6 minutes active grilling** after preheat, charred edges, warm smoky centers—veggie “steak.”

***

## 1. Prep the wedges (10 minutes before grilling)

- **Choose the veg:**  
  - **2 heads romaine** (halved lengthwise) or **1 medium cabbage** (cut into 6–8 wedges through core).  
- **Seasoning:**  
  - Olive oil, kosher salt, black pepper.  

**Do’s**  
- Trim loose outer leaves, cut through core to hold wedges together.  
- Brush cut sides generously with oil + salt + pepper.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t cut too thin; wedges need structure.  
- Don’t oil too heavily; flare‑ups.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Direct **high (450–550°F)**.
- **Fire setup:**
  - **Charcoal pan in high position.**
  - **Full bed** of charcoal across grate (1.5 chimney loads, even layer). [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Fully open** for max airflow. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 10–15 minutes** lid closed until lid thermometer ~500°F.
- Brush grates clean while hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t preheat too long; quick cook.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Direct **high (450–550°F)**.
- **Weber Spirit II setup:**
  - **E‑310:** All three burners HIGH.
  - **E‑210:** Both burners HIGH.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners HIGH, lid closed **10–15 min** until ~500°F.
- **Oil the cut sides of the veg**, not the grates.

**Do’s**
- Cast iron grates preheated to HIGH give romaine and cabbage clean char marks in 2 min.
- This is a 4–6 min cook; stay at the grill the whole time.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t preheat less than 10 min; cabbage won’t release cleanly from grates.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the wedges on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Direct over coals** across hot grate.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - **Cut‑side down** first for char.  

**Do’s**  
- Grill **2 minutes cut‑side down**, flip to **other cut side 1–2 min**, then **round side 1 min**.  
- Use **long tongs**; look for charred edges.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overcrowd; steaming dulls char.  
- Don’t flip too early; crust needs time.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (450–550°F direct)

**Goal:** Charred exterior, warm tender core. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Veg | Total time | Visual cue |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Romaine wedges** | 4–5 min | Charred cut sides, wilted leaves. |
| **Cabbage wedges** | 5–6 min | Charred edges, softened core. |

**Do’s**  
- Check after first flip; pull when charred/tender.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t let core burn through.  

***

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Drizzle **olive oil**, squeeze **lemon**, sprinkle **parmesan or cotija** + chili flakes.  
- Serve with **your smoked salsa** or **chimichurri**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t dress before grilling; burns.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for grilled romaine/cabbage wedges on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **full high grate** and **lid thermometer** ~500°F.  
- Oil cut sides, grill **cut‑down first**.  
- **Lid open** for char.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip preheat; soft wedges.  
- Don’t overcrowd; no char.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **2–4 wedges:** Same timing.  
- **8+ wedges:** Batches.  

Veggie steak vibes.
