**Step‑by‑step guide** for **spatchcock chicken quarters**, tuned for **4 chicken quarters** (legs + breasts) using **two‑zone fire**. Total time: **35–45 minutes active grilling** after preheat, crispy skin, juicy meat.

***

## 1. Prep the chicken quarters (45–60 minutes before grilling)

- **Choose the quarters:**  
  - 4 **bone‑in chicken quarters** (legs + thighs attached to breasts; ~1–1.5 lb each).  
- **Spatchcock/spatch each quarter:**  
  - Cut along backbone of each quarter, press flat to crack breastbone.  
- **Seasoning:**  
  - Kosher salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika (1 tsp salt + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1 tsp garlic + 1 tsp paprika per pound).  

**Do’s**  
- Pat quarters dry with paper towels.  
- Rub generously under and over skin; let sit at room temp for **45–60 minutes**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip spatchcocking; ensures even cooking.  
- Don’t add wet marinades; soggy skin.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **medium (350–400°F)** overall.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in mid position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 1.5 chimney loads of charcoal piled under **half** the grate.
  - **Cool zone:** No charcoal under the other half. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Mostly open** during preheat and cook. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 20 minutes** with lid closed until lid thermometer reads ~375°F.
- Brush grates clean while hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip preheat; sticking kills skin.
- Don’t spread charcoal evenly; two‑zone crisps without burning.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Indirect **350–400°F**, then crisp at HIGH.
- **Weber Spirit II two‑zone (indirect-first):**
  - **E‑310:** Left burner MEDIUM (direct/crisp zone). Center + right burners OFF (indirect). Quarters start over center/right.
  - **E‑210:** Left burner MEDIUM. Right burner OFF. Quarters start over right.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners HIGH, lid closed **10–15 min**, dial to pattern. Hit ~375°F on indirect side.

**Do’s**
- Indirect phase **lid closed**—the Spirit II acts like an oven, cooking quarters evenly from all sides.
- Crisp phase: move to HIGH‑burner side, skin‑down, lid open 3–4 min.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip indirect phase; going straight to high heat chars skin before meat cooks through.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the quarters on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Cool/indirect zone first** (no coals under) to cook through gently.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - **Skin‑side up**, spatchcocked flat.  

**Do’s**  
- Cook indirect **15–20 minutes** until ~140°F internal, then move to hot zone for crisp.  
- Rotate/flip as needed for even cooking.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t start skin‑down indirect; renders fat too slow.  
- Don’t overcrowd; needs space.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (350–400°F two‑zone)

**Goal:** Internal **165°F** breast/thigh, crispy skin. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Phase | Time | Internal temp target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Indirect cook (cool zone):** 20–25 min | Lid closed, rotate halfway | 140–145°F |
| **Hot crisp (hot zone):** 10–15 min | Skin‑down first 3–4 min, then skin‑up | Pull at 165°F |

**Do’s**  
- Check internal temp **every 7–10 minutes** indirect.  
- Use a **fast‑read thermometer** in thickest thigh part.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t rush indirect; undercooked chicken is risky.  
- Don’t overshoot 170°F; dries out.  

***

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest quarters **5–7 minutes** on a plate tented loosely with foil.  
- Serve with **Alabama white sauce, ranch, or chimichurri**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t cut early; juices redistribute.  
- Don’t tent tightly; keeps skin crisp.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for spatchcock chicken quarters on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **two‑zone fire** and **lid thermometer** to confirm ~375°F.  
- Spatchcock flat, indirect first for even cook.  
- Crisp skin **lid open** on hot zone.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip preheat or dry chicken; soggy skin.  
- Don’t overshoot 170°F; dries fast.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **2 quarters:** Shave 5–7 min indirect.  
- **6 quarters:** Stagger hot zone by 2–3 min.  

Whole‑bird flavor, faster cook.
