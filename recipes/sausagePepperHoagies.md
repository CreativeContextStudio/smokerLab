**Step‑by‑step guide** for **sausage & pepper hoagies**, tuned for **Italian sausage links + bell peppers/onions** using **two‑zone fire**. Total time: **20–25 minutes active grilling** after preheat, charred casings, juicy filling.

***

## 1. Prep the sausage & peppers (15–30 minutes before grilling)

- **Choose the sausage:**  
  - 6–8 **Italian sausage links** (sweet or hot, about 4–6 oz each).  
- **Veggies:**  
  - 2–3 **bell peppers** (any color, 1–1.5" wide strips).  
  - 1 large **yellow onion** (1" wedges).  
- **Hoagie rolls:**  
  - 6–8 soft hoagie/sub rolls.  

**Do’s**  
- No need to prick sausages; keeps juices in.  
- Toss peppers/onions lightly in oil + salt + pepper.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t parboil sausages; dries them out.  
- Don’t overcrowd skewers if using; space for char.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **medium (350–400°F)** overall.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in mid position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 1.5 chimney loads of charcoal piled under **half** the grate.
  - **Cool zone:** No charcoal under the other half. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Mostly open** during preheat and cook. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 15–20 minutes** with lid closed until lid thermometer reads ~375°F.
- Brush grates clean while hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip preheat; sticking kills char.
- Don’t spread charcoal evenly; two‑zone finishes sausages gently.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **medium (350–400°F)** overall.
- **Weber Spirit II two‑zone:**
  - **E‑310:** Left burner MEDIUM‑HIGH (sear). Center burner LOW. Right burner LOW (indirect finish). Veggies on right side throughout.
  - **E‑210:** Left burner MEDIUM‑HIGH (sear). Right burner LOW (indirect).
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners HIGH, lid closed **10–15 min**, then dial to pattern. Hit ~375°F.

**Do’s**
- Place veggies on the low‑burner side from the start—they take the same 15–20 min.
- Sear sausages on direct side, then slide to low indirect side to finish.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t pierce sausages; juice loss on both gas and charcoal.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the sausage & peppers on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Sausages:** Hot zone first (over coals) for char.  
  - **Peppers/onions:** Cool zone (indirect) or edge of hot.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - Sausages lay flat; peppers skin‑side down.  

**Do’s**  
- Cook sausages **indirect after 6–8 min** searing to finish gently.  
- Rotate sausages/peppers for even char.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t pierce sausages; juice loss.  
- Don’t overcrowd hot zone.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (350–400°F two‑zone)

**Goal:** Sausages **160°F internal**, peppers/onions charred/soft. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Item | Phase | Time | Internal/visual |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Sausages** | Sear hot zone | 6–8 min | Golden char |
| | Indirect finish | 8–12 min | 160°F |
| **Peppers/onions** | Direct or edge | 15–20 min total | Soft, charred |

**Do’s**  
- Check sausage temp **after searing**.  
- Use a **fast‑read thermometer** in thickest part.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overshoot 165°F on sausages; dries out.  

***

## 5. Minute‑by‑minute cook script (6–8 sausages + veggies, 350–400°F two‑zone)

| Time since grill preheat | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| **Preheat:** 15–20 min | Lid closed, coals under half grate, vents mostly open, hit ~375°F lid temp.  [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850) |

| Time since grill preheat | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| **Preheat:** 10–15 min | All burners HIGH, lid closed, dial to two‑zone pattern, hit ~375°F. |
| **0–4 min:** Sausages over MEDIUM‑HIGH burner, lid open. Veggies on LOW side. |
| **4–8 min:** Rotate sausages, stir veggies. Slide sausages to LOW side. |

**Target finish:** Sausages 160°F, veggies charred/tender.  

***

## 6. Rest, assemble, and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest sausages **3–5 minutes**.  
- Toast hoagie rolls **direct 1 min**.  
- Split sausages lengthwise, pile in rolls with peppers/onions.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t cut sausages early; juices run.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for sausage & pepper hoagies on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **two‑zone fire** and **lid thermometer** to confirm ~375°F.  
- Sear sausages hot, finish indirect.  
- Toast rolls at end.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t pierce sausages; dry meat.  
- Don’t overcrowd hot zone.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **4 sausages:** Shave 2–3 min.  
- **8+ sausages:** Stagger hot zone.
