Here’s a **tight, step‑by‑step guide** for **pizza night**, tuned for **3–4 personal pizzas (10–12")**. Total time: **2–5 minutes per pizza** after preheat, blistered crust, melty cheese—wood‑fired vibes without oven.

***

## 1. Prep the pizzas (1 hour before grilling)

- **Dough:**  
  - **Store‑bought or homemade** (high‑hydration for puff), divided into **10–12" balls**, rest 30–60 min room temp.  
- **Sauce/toppings:**  
  - Thin **tomato sauce**, fresh mozz (torn), pepperoni/sausage/veggies.  

**Do’s**  
- Stretch dough thin on floured surface; **par‑bake optional** (oven 10 min).  
- Prep toppings light; pre‑cook meats.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overload; soggy flop.  
- Don’t use wet toppings first.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **ultra‑high (550–700°F)** overall.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in high position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 2–2.5 chimney loads piled under **one side** (or ring around edges).
  - **Cool zone:** Empty side/center for finish. [royalgourmetcorp](https://www.royalgourmetcorp.com/pdfs/item/2020/20200922/45bdea1a-4c30-4965-976d-e9fea0aa2fa4__CC1830S.pdf)
- **Vents:**
  - **Fully open** top/bottom for max heat. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 20–30 minutes** lid closed until lid thermometer maxes (~600°F+); coals glowing.
- Clean/oil grates hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skimp coals; low heat = doughy.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** **500–550°F** (Spirit II max—not 700°F like loaded charcoal, but enough for pizza).
- **Weber Spirit II setup:**
  - **E‑310:** All three burners HIGH, lid closed 15–20 min. If using a pizza stone, place it now and let it preheat fully.
  - **E‑210:** Both burners HIGH. Pizza stone strongly recommended to compensate for lower max temp.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Pizza stone:** Place on grate before lighting, preheat with grill for full 15–20 min. This is essential on gas to get bottom crust crisp.

**Do’s**
- A pizza stone preheated on the Spirit II gives the closest result to wood‑fired. Without it, crust may be pale on the bottom.
- Thin dough, light toppings—gas can’t recover heat as fast as charcoal if you overload.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t expect 700°F; the Spirit II tops out around 550°F. Pizzas will take slightly longer (3–5 min vs 2–4 min).
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the pizza on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Hot zone** dough‑side down first.  
- **Tools:**  
  - **Pizza peel** (floured/cornmeal), tongs.  

**Do’s**  
- Slide dough hot zone; **lid down 45–60 sec** first side.  
- Flip to **cool zone**, add toppings, **lid down 1–2 min**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t open lid constantly; lose heat.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (550–700°F two‑zone)

**Goal:** Blistered crust, bubbly cheese. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AvRwqbceHHU)

| Stage | Time | Visual cue |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Dough side 1** | 45–90 sec | Puffed, grill marks. |
| **Topped finish** | 1.5–3 min | Cheese melted/browned.  [playswellwithbutter](https://playswellwithbutter.com/grilled-pizza-recipe/) |

**Do’s**  
- Rotate for even char.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t burn bottom; watch coals.  

***

## 5. Minute‑by‑minute cook script (per 10–12" pizza)

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
| Time since grill preheat | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| **Preheat:** 20–30 min | Lid closed, coals piled hot side, vents fully open, hit 600°F+.  [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850) |
| **0–1 min (Dough sear):** Stretch/slide dough hot zone, lid down. |
| **1 min (Flip):** Peel flip to cool zone, sauce/cheese/toppings quick. |
| **1–4 min (Bake):** Lid down, rotate, pull bubbly/charred.  [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AvRwqbceHHU) |
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
| Time since grill preheat | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| **Preheat:** 15–20 min | All burners HIGH + pizza stone on grate, lid closed, hit ~550°F. |
| **0–1.5 min (Dough side 1):** Slide dough onto hot stone or grate, lid closed. |
| **1.5 min (Flip or top):** Flip bare dough, quickly add sauce/cheese/toppings. |
| **2–5 min (Bake):** Lid closed. Rotate 180° at 2 min. Pull when cheese bubbles/browns. |
<!-- /equipment -->

**Target finish:** Blistered, smoky.  

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest on wire rack **2 min**; slice.  
- Drizzle **olive oil**, fresh basil.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t cut hot; soggy.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for pizza night on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- **Two‑zone ultra‑high** ~600°F, thin dough.  
- **Lid down** most time; peel mastery. [playswellwithbutter](https://playswellwithbutter.com/grilled-pizza-recipe/)
- Light toppings.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overload; falls apart.  
- Don’t preheat short; raw center.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **Personal size:** 10–12".  
- **2+ pizzas:** Stagger, keep dough warm.  

Pizza perfection.
