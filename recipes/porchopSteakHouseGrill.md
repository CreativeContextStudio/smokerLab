**Step‑by‑step guide** for **pork chop “steakhouse”**, tuned for **1.25” bone‑in pork loin chops** using **two‑zone fire**. Total time: **12–16 minutes active grilling** after preheat, steakhouse crust with juicy pink center.

***

## 1. Prep the pork chops (30–60 minutes before grilling)

- **Choose the chops:**  
  - 1.25" thick **bone‑in pork loin chops** (about 10–12 oz each).  
- **Seasoning:**  
  - Kosher salt, coarse black pepper, garlic powder (1 tsp salt + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp garlic powder per chop).  

**Do’s**  
- Pat chops dry with paper towels.  
- Season generously on both sides; let sit at room temp for **30–60 minutes**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t add oil or sauces yet; they burn during sear.  
- Don’t trim fat; it renders and crisps.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **medium‑high (400–450°F)** overall.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in high or mid position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 1.5–2 chimney loads of charcoal piled under **half** the grate.
  - **Cool zone:** No charcoal under the other half. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Mostly open** during preheat and cook for clean airflow. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 15–20 minutes** with lid closed until lid thermometer reads ~425°F.
- Brush grates clean while hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip preheat; cold grates stick.
- Don’t spread charcoal evenly; two‑zone gives crust + finish control.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **medium‑high (400–450°F)** overall.
- **Weber Spirit II two‑zone:**
  - **E‑310:** Left burner HIGH (sear). Center + right burners OFF (indirect zone). Chops finish over center/right.
  - **E‑210:** Left burner HIGH (sear). Right burner OFF (indirect). Chops finish over right.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners HIGH, lid closed **10–15 min** until ~450°F, then set to two‑zone pattern.

**Do’s**
- Sear **lid open** over HIGH burner for crust. Indirect finish **lid closed**—gas oven‑style heat finishes chops perfectly.
- Bone‑in chops are thick enough to hold in indirect without overcooking.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip the indirect finish; 1.25" chops need the gentle close to hit 135–140°F safely.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the chops on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Hot zone first** (over coals) for crust.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - Lay chops flat, **bone toward edge** for even cooking.  

**Do’s**  
- Sear **2–3 minutes per side** until deep golden crust forms.  
- Move to **cool/indirect zone** after searing, lid closed to finish.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t crowd; space allows fat to render.  
- Don’t press down; let crust build naturally.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (400–450°F two‑zone)

**Goal:** Internal **135–140°F** for pink, juicy center (carryover hits 145°F). [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Phase | Time | Internal temp target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Sear (hot zone):** 4–6 min total | 2–3 min per side | 100–110°F |
| **Indirect finish (cool zone):** 6–8 min | Lid closed, no flip needed | Pull at 135–140°F |

**Do’s**  
- Check internal temp **after searing**, then every 3–4 minutes indirect.  
- Use a **fast‑read thermometer** in thickest part, away from bone.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overshoot 145°F; dries out fast.  
- Don’t flip during indirect; let carryover work.  

***

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest chops **5 minutes** on a plate tented loosely with foil (carryover hits 145°F).  
- Serve with your **chimichurri, ranch, or Alabama white sauce**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t cut early; juices need to redistribute.  
- Don’t tent tightly; crust stays crisp.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for pork chop “steakhouse” on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **two‑zone fire** and **lid thermometer** to confirm ~425°F overall.  
- Pat dry and season simply for crust.  
- Sear **lid open**, indirect **lid closed**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip preheat or dry chops; no crust without it.  
- Don’t overshoot 140°F; pink juicy is the goal.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **2 chops:** Same timing.  
- **4+ chops:** Stagger searing by 2 min batches.
