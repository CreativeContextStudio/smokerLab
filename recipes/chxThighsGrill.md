**Step‑by‑step guide** for **bone‑in, skin‑on chicken thighs**, tuned for **6–8 thighs** using **two‑zone fire** (sear then indirect). Total time: **25–35 minutes active grilling** after preheat, crispy skin, juicy inside.

***

## 1. Prep the chicken thighs (30–60 minutes before grilling)

- **Choose the thighs:**  
  - 6–8 bone‑in, skin‑on chicken thighs (about 4–6 oz each).  
- **Simple rub:**  
  - 1 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp paprika, pinch of sugar per pound.  

**Do’s**  
- Pat thighs dry with paper towels.  
- Rub generously under and over skin; let sit at room temp for **30–60 minutes**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t add wet marinades; they make skin soggy.  
- Don’t trim skin; it protects and crisps.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **medium (350–400°F)** overall.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in mid position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 1–1.5 chimney loads of charcoal piled under **half** the grate.
  - **Cool zone:** No charcoal under the other half. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Mostly open** during preheat and cook for clean airflow. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 15–20 minutes** with lid closed until lid thermometer reads ~375°F.
- Brush grates clean while hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip preheat; cold grates stick skin.
- Don’t spread charcoal evenly; two‑zone is essential for crispy skin without burning.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **medium (350–400°F)** overall.
- **Weber Spirit II two‑zone:**
  - **E‑310:** Left burner MEDIUM‑HIGH (sear zone). Center burner LOW. Right burner LOW or OFF (indirect).
  - **E‑210:** Left burner MEDIUM‑HIGH. Right burner LOW.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners HIGH, lid closed **10–15 min**, dial to pattern. Hit ~375°F.

**Do’s**
- Start thighs **skin‑side down** on the MEDIUM‑HIGH burner side; fat renders into Flavorizer bars for flavor.
- Indirect finish **lid closed** for steady convection heat to 165°F.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t rush the sear; skin needs 3–4 min uninterrupted to release cleanly from cast iron grates.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the thighs on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Hot zone first** (over coals) to render fat and crisp skin.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - **Skin‑side down** for first sear.  

**Do’s**  
- Sear **skin‑down 3–4 minutes** until golden, then flip.  
- Move to **cool/indirect zone** after 6–8 minutes total searing, lid closed.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t crowd; 6–8 thighs need space.  
- Don’t press down; let fat render naturally.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (350–400°F two‑zone)

**Goal:** Internal **165–170°F**, skin crispy, juices clear. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Phase | Time | Internal temp target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Sear (hot zone):** 6–8 min total | Skin‑down 3–4 min, flip, 3–4 min | 120–130°F |
| **Indirect finish (cool zone):** 15–20 min | Lid closed, flip once halfway | Pull at 165–170°F |

**Do’s**  
- Check internal temp **every 5–7 minutes** after searing.  
- Use a **fast‑read thermometer** in thickest part, away from bone.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t rush sear; crispy skin takes time.  
- Don’t overshoot 175°F; dries out fast.  

***

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest thighs **5–7 minutes** on a plate tented loosely with foil.  
- Serve with your **Alabama white sauce, ranch, or chimichurri**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t cut early; juices need to redistribute.  
- Don’t sauce too early; let skin stay crisp.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for bone‑in chicken thighs on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **two‑zone fire** and **lid thermometer** to confirm ~375°F overall.  
- Pat dry and season simply for crispy skin.  
- Sear **lid open**, indirect **lid closed**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip preheat or dry thighs; soggy skin ruins it.  
- Don’t crowd hot zone; fat renders better with space.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **4 thighs:** Shave 3–5 min off indirect.  
- **8+ thighs:** Stagger searing by 2–3 min batches.  

This gives you **crispy skin, juicy thighs**, your style exactly.
