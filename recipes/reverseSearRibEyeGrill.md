**Step‑by‑step guide** for **reverse‑sear ribeye**, tuned for **1.25" thick ribeyes** using **two‑zone fire**. Total time: **20–30 minutes active grilling** after preheat, steakhouse quality.

***

## 1. Prep the ribeyes (30–60 minutes before grilling)

- **Choose the ribeyes:**  
  - 1.25" thick ribeye steaks (about 12–16 oz each).  
- **Seasoning:**  
  - Kosher salt and coarse black pepper only (1 tsp salt + 1/2 tsp pepper per pound).  

**Do’s**  
- Pat steaks dry with paper towels.  
- Season generously on both sides and let sit at room temp for **30–60 minutes**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t add oil or sauces yet; they can burn during the sear.  
- Don’t salt too early; it draws out moisture.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Indirect low‑medium **325–375°F**, then hot sear **450–550°F**.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in mid or high position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 1–1.5 chimney loads of charcoal piled under **half** the grate.
  - **Cool zone:** No charcoal under the other half. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Mostly open** during preheat and cook for clean airflow. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 20–30 minutes** with lid closed until lid thermometer reads ~350°F.
- Use a **grate probe** if you have one to confirm grate temp in indirect zone.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip the preheat; cold grates stick and cook unevenly.
- Don’t spread charcoal evenly; two‑zone is key for reverse sear.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Indirect **325–375°F**, then sear **450–550°F**.
- **Weber Spirit II two‑zone:**
  - **E‑310:** Left burner HIGH (sear zone). Center burner LOW (buffer). Right burner OFF (indirect zone). Steaks go over right.
  - **E‑210:** Left burner HIGH (sear zone). Right burner OFF (indirect zone). Steaks go over right.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners HIGH 10–15 min, then dial back to target pattern. Lid closed, hit ~350°F on indirect side.

**Do’s**
- Indirect phase: lid **closed** the whole time. Gas oven‑style convection finishes steaks evenly.
- Sear phase: move steaks to HIGH burner side, lid **open** for crust control.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip preheat; cast iron grates need 10–15 min to fully heat.
- Don’t open lid during indirect phase; heat escapes fast on gas.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the ribeyes on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Cool/indirect zone** first (no coals under them).  
- **Orientation:**  
  - Lay steaks flat, **not touching each other**.  

**Do’s**  
- Close the lid and cook **indirect until internal temp hits 115°F** (rare) or 120°F (medium‑rare).  
- Rotate 90° halfway through each side for even cooking.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t put them over coals yet; that’s the sear phase.  
- Don’t open the lid constantly; keep heat stable.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (325–375°F indirect)

**Goal:** Internal **115–120°F** before searing (carryover will finish them). [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Steak thickness | Indirect time (lid closed) | Internal temp target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1.25" ribeye | 8–12 minutes per side | 115°F (rare) or 120°F (medium‑rare) |

**Do’s**  
- Check internal temp **every 4–5 minutes** after the first 8 minutes.  
- Use a **fast‑read thermometer** in the thickest part, away from bone/fat.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overshoot the internal temp; searing adds 10–15°F carryover.  
- Don’t flip too often; let each side cook fully.  

***

**Target finish:** **115–120°F internal** before searing.  

***

## 6. Hot sear finish (450–550°F direct zone)

- **Move steaks to hot zone** (over coals).  
- **Sear 1–2 minutes per side** until deep crust forms.  
- **Pull at 130°F (medium‑rare) or 135°F (medium)**; carryover hits 135°F and 140°F resting.  

**Do’s**  
- Sear **lid open** for control and crust.  
- Use **long tongs** to flip; don’t press down.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t walk away; sear happens fast.  
- Don’t overshoot; crust is visual, not temp.  

***

## 7. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest steaks **5–7 minutes** on a plate tented with foil.  
- Slice against the grain, serve with your **chimichurri, garlic aioli, or ranch**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t cut early; juices need to redistribute.  
- Don’t wrap tightly; you want a little crust breath.  

***

## 8. Do’s / Don’ts for reverse‑sear ribeye on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **two‑zone fire** and **lid thermometer** to confirm indirect zone ~350°F.  
- Season simply and let steaks come to room temp.  
- Sear **lid open** for crust control.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip preheat or dry steaks; sticking kills crust.  
- Don’t chase exact grate temps without a probe; lid thermometer + visual is enough.  

**Scale notes:**  
- For **2 steaks:** Same timing.  
- For **4 steaks:** Stagger indirect by 4–5 min to manage sear zone space.  

This gives you **steakhouse crust + perfect internal**, no fancy gear needed.
