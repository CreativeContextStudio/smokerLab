**Step‑by‑step guide** for **tuna or swordfish steaks**, tuned for **1–1.25" thick steaks** over **direct high heat**. Total time: **6–10 minutes active grilling** after preheat, rare to medium‑rare center with seared crust.

***

## 1. Prep the seafood steaks (15–30 minutes before grilling)

- **Choose the steaks:**  
  - 1–1.25" thick **tuna steaks** (ahi or yellowfin) or **swordfish steaks** (about 6–8 oz each).  
- **Seasoning:**  
  - Kosher salt, coarse black pepper, garlic powder (light: 1/2 tsp salt + 1/4 tsp pepper per steak).  
  - Neutral oil (avocado or grapeseed) for brushing.  

**Do’s**  
- Pat steaks dry with paper towels.  
- Brush lightly with oil, season both sides; let sit at room temp for **15–30 minutes**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t use olive oil; it smokes too fast.  
- Don’t salt too early or heavily; fish is delicate.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **high (450–550°F)** overall.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in high position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 1.5–2 chimney loads of charcoal piled under **half** the grate.
  - **Cool zone:** No charcoal under the other half. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Fully open** during preheat and sear for max airflow. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 15–20 minutes** with lid closed until lid thermometer reads ~500°F.
- Brush grates clean while hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip preheat; cold grates stick fish.
- Don’t spread charcoal evenly; two‑zone lets you control doneness.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Direct **high (450–550°F)**.
- **Weber Spirit II setup:**
  - **E‑310:** All burners HIGH. Right burner LOW as rescue zone if steaks need more time.
  - **E‑210:** Both burners HIGH.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners HIGH, lid closed **10–15 min** until lid thermometer ~500°F.
- **Oil the fish**, not the grates.

**Do’s**
- Cast iron grates on the Spirit II give a clean sear on fish when properly preheated.
- If steaks start to stick, they need 30 more seconds—don’t force the flip.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t use olive oil; it smokes at these temps. Avocado or grapeseed only.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the steaks on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Hot zone** (over coals) for sear.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - Lay steaks flat, **thicker edge toward heat center**.  

**Do’s**  
- Sear **1.5–2 minutes per side** for rare crust, or 2–2.5 minutes for medium‑rare.  
- Move to **cool zone** if you want gentler finish.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overcrowd; space prevents steaming.  
- Don’t press down; let crust form naturally.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (450–550°F two‑zone)

**Goal:** Internal **110–115°F (rare)** or **120–125°F (medium‑rare)**. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Doneness | Sear time per side | Internal temp pull |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Rare** | 1.5–2 min | 110–115°F |
| **Medium‑rare** | 2–2.5 min | 120–125°F |

**Do’s**  
- Check internal temp **after first flip**.  
- Use a **fast‑read thermometer** in thickest part.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overshoot 130°F; dries out fast.  
- Don’t flip too early; crust needs time.  

***

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest steaks **3–5 minutes** on a plate (minimal carryover).  
- Slice against grain, serve with **chimichurri, garlic aioli, or cilantro‑lime crema**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t over‑rest; seafood likes to be warm.  
- Don’t sauce before serving; keeps crust crisp.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for tuna/swordfish steaks on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **two‑zone fire** and **lid thermometer** to confirm ~500°F.  
- Oil steaks (not grates), season lightly.  
- Sear **lid open** for crust control.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip preheat or dry steaks; no crust without it.  
- Don’t go past 125°F internal; overcooked fish is sad.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **2 steaks:** Same timing.  
- **4 steaks:** Stagger searing by 1–2 min batches.  

Rare crust, pink center, steakhouse seafood.
