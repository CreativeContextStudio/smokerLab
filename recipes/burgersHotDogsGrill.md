Here’s a **tight, step‑by‑step guide** for **all‑American burgers and hotdogs**, tuned for **8–12 burgers (⅓ lb each) + 8–12 dogs**. Total time: **10–15 minutes active grilling** after preheat, smashed crusty burgers, snappy grilled dogs—classic cookout.

***

## 1. Prep the burgers and hotdogs (20 minutes before grilling)

- **Burgers:**  
  - **2–3 lb 80/20 ground beef** (loosely formed into **⅓–½ lb balls**, not patties).  
- **Hotdogs:**  
  - **All‑beef dogs** (standard or jumbo).  
- **Seasoning:**  
  - Kosher salt + black pepper.  

**Do’s**  
- **Chill beef** 30 min; form **loose balls** (thumbprint center).  
- Pat dogs dry. Season burgers **just before grill**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overwork beef; tough burgers.  
- Don’t pre‑salt beef; draws moisture.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **high (450–550°F)** overall.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in high position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 2 chimney loads piled under **half** grate.
  - **Cool zone:** No charcoal under other half. [royalgourmetcorp](https://www.royalgourmetcorp.com/pdfs/item/2020/20200922/45bdea1a-4c30-4965-976d-e9fea0aa2fa4__CC1830S.pdf)
- **Vents:**
  - **Fully open** for max airflow. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 15–20 minutes** lid closed until lid thermometer ~500°F.
- Brush grates clean + oil lightly.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t preheat low; no crust.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **high (450–550°F)** overall.
- **Weber Spirit II setup:**
  - **E‑310:** Left + center burners HIGH; right burner OFF (cool zone for dog finishing).
  - **E‑210:** Left burner HIGH; right burner OFF.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners HIGH, lid closed **10–15 minutes** until lid thermometer ~500°F.
- Brush grates clean; **oil the food, not the grates**.

**Do’s**
- Cast iron grates hold heat well—smash technique works great on the Spirit II.
- A cast iron press helps maximize crust if you have one.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip preheat; cold cast iron won’t build crust.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place burgers and hotdogs on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Burgers:** Hot zone first for smash.  
  - **Hotdogs:** Hot zone for char, move to cool.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - Burgers ball‑side down; dogs flat.  

**Do’s**  
- **Smash burgers** immediately (1–2 min/side).  
- Dogs **roll every 1–2 min**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t press burgers after smash; dry out.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (450–550°F two‑zone)

**Goal:** Burgers **medium 140–150°F**, dogs heated/snappy. [heygrillhey](https://heygrillhey.com/grilled-burgers/)

| Item | Total time | Internal/visual |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Burgers (⅓ lb)** | 4–6 min | 140–150°F (juicy pink), crusty edges.  [heygrillhey](https://heygrillhey.com/grilled-burgers/) |
| **Hotdogs** | 6–8 min | 160°F or split/snappy.  [royalgourmetcorp](https://www.royalgourmetcorp.com/pdfs/item/2020/20200922/45bdea1a-4c30-4965-976d-e9fea0aa2fa4__CC1830S.pdf) |

**Do’s**  
- Check burgers after 3 min; dogs by feel.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overshoot burgers 160°F.  

***

## 5. Minute‑by‑minute cook script (8–12 burgers + dogs, 450–550°F two‑zone)

**Target finish:** Crusty, juicy.  

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Burgers rest **1–2 min** (toasted buns on cool zone).  
- Serve with **American cheese, pickles, onions, ketchup/mustard**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t stack cold; steam kills crisp.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for burgers/hotdogs on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- **Two‑zone high fire** ~500°F, **lid thermometer**.  
- Smash burgers, roll dogs.  
- **Lid open** for char. [royalgourmetcorp](https://www.royalgourmetcorp.com/pdfs/item/2020/20200922/45bdea1a-4c30-4965-976d-e9fea0aa2fa4__CC1830S.pdf)

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t press after smash; dry.  
- Don’t overcrowd; steaming.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **4–6 burgers:** Shave 1 min.  
- **12+:** 2 batches.  

Cookout complete.
