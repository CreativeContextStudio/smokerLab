**Step‑by‑step guide** for **kabob party platter**, tuned for **chicken, pork, shrimp, veggies** using **direct medium heat**. Total time: **12–18 minutes active grilling** after preheat, charred crusts, juicy centers—feed 4–6 easily.

***

## 1. Prep the kabobs (30 minutes before grilling)

- **Proteins & veggies (for 8–12 skewers):**  
  - **Chicken:** 1.25 lb boneless thighs, 1.5" cubes.  
  - **Pork:** 1 lb tenderloin or loin, 1.5" cubes.  
  - **Shrimp:** 1 lb large (16/20), peeled/deveined.  
  - **Veggies:** 2 zucchini (1" rounds), 2 bell peppers (1.5" chunks), 1 red onion (1.5" wedges).  
- **Seasoning:**  
  - Oil + salt/pepper/garlic powder for all. Optional herb brush.  

**Do’s**  
- Cube uniformly **1–1.5"** pieces.  
- Marinate lightly 20–30 min: 3 tbsp oil + 1 tsp salt + 1 tsp pepper + 1 tsp garlic per pound.  
- Thread **alternating** types (e.g., chicken + zucchini + onion) on **metal skewers**.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overcrowd skewers; needs space to char.  
- Don’t use wood skewers; burn fast.  

***

## 2. Set up the grill

<!-- equipment: charcoal-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Two‑zone **medium (375–425°F)** overall.
- **Fire setup (two‑zone):**
  - **Charcoal pan in mid position.**
  - **Hot zone:** 1.5 chimney loads piled under **half** grate.
  - **Cool zone:** No charcoal under other half. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)
- **Vents:**
  - **Mostly open** during preheat and cook. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

**Do’s**
- **Preheat 15 minutes** lid closed until lid thermometer ~400°F.
- Brush grates clean while hot.

**Don’ts**
- Don’t skip preheat; sticking kills char.
<!-- /equipment -->

<!-- equipment: gas-grill -->
- **Target temp:** Direct **medium (375–425°F)** across full grate.
- **Weber Spirit II setup:**
  - **E‑310:** All three burners MEDIUM‑HIGH. Right burner LOW as shrimp finishing zone.
  - **E‑210:** Both burners MEDIUM‑HIGH. Slide shrimp to edge of right burner to finish.
  - Light with lid **open**, then close to preheat.
- **Preheat:** All burners MEDIUM‑HIGH, lid closed **10–15 min** until ~400°F.
- Brush grates; **oil the skewers, not the grates**.

**Do’s**
- Full‑grate even heat is ideal for kabobs—no need to shuffle between zones constantly.
- Pull shrimp early (4–6 min) before chicken (10–12 min).

**Don’ts**
- Don’t overcrowd; steam kills char on gas too.
<!-- /equipment -->  

***

## 3. Place the kabobs on the grill

- **Position:**  
  - **Hot zone first** 2–3 min per side for char.  
  - Move to **cool zone** to finish if needed.  
- **Orientation:**  
  - Lay skewers flat, **not overlapping**.  

**Do’s**  
- Grill **2–3 minutes per side** (3–4 rotations total).  
- Shrimp skewers **shorter time** (1.5–2 min/side).  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overcrowd; steaming.  
- Don’t flip too often; char needs time.  

***

## 4. Grill time and temp targets (375–425°F two‑zone)

**Goal:** Chicken/pork **160–165°F**, shrimp opaque, veggies charred/tender. [manuals](https://manuals.plus/m/db251b7540fb780b412c58fcea4064c10d3102e8641e3cf051440a2357e13850)

| Kabob type | Total time | Internal/visual |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Chicken** | 10–12 min | 160–165°F |
| **Pork** | 10–12 min | 145°F (pink) or 160°F |
| **Shrimp** | 4–6 min | Opaque pink |
| **Veggie** | 8–10 min | Charred, fork‑tender |

**Do’s**  
- Check proteins after 8 min; veggies by feel.  
- **Fast‑read thermometer** for meats.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t overshoot proteins.  

***

## 5. Minute‑by‑minute cook script (8–12 kabobs, 375–425°F)

**Target finish:** Charred, cooked through.  

***

## 6. Rest and serve

**Do’s**  
- Rest kabobs **2–3 minutes** off heat.  
- Serve with **chimichurri, ranch, or Alabama white** + lemon.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t over‑rest; cools fast.  

***

## 7. Do’s / Don’ts for kabob party platter on CC1830S main grill

**Do’s**  
- Use **two‑zone medium fire** and **lid thermometer** ~400°F.  
- Uniform cubes, stagger doneness.  
- **Lid open** for char.  

**Don’ts**  
- Don’t skip preheat; no crust.  
- Don’t overcrowd; steaming.  

**Scale notes:**  
- **4–6 kabobs:** Shave 2 min.  
- **12+ kabobs:** 2 batches.  

Party ready.
